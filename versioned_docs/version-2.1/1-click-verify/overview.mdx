## About 1-Click Verify

**1-Click Verify is our dedicated solution for verifying user phone numbers,** and soon emails and more. Verifying user phone numbers is necessary to use our flagship [1-Click Signup](/1-click-signup) product, so 1-Click Verify provides a powerful and easy to integrate solution.

:::tip 
If you already verify user phone numbers with another provider or your own solution, that's totally fine. You can still use our other Verified products, like [1-Click Signup](/1-click-signup).
:::


## SMS Compliance

SMS sending in the US is subject to onerous compliance reviews by SMS platforms and phone carriers. We handle all of this complexity, so you don't have to. 


## Benefits

- **Easily verify user phone numbers:** 1-Click Verify makes it extremely easy to verify user phone numbers, with minimal integration work.
- **Seamlessly integrate with 1-Click Signup and 1-Click Health:** Phone verification is the prerequisite for using our flagship [1-Click Signup](/1-click-signup) product, so 1-Click Verify can smoothly flow into 1-Click Signup (which can then flow into 1-Click Health).


## Coverage

1-Click Verify works for 99% of US adults (264M people): anyone with a cell phone!

:::important
**We currently only cover people in the United States,** but we're soon adding new geographies. If there are particular geographies that would be most helpful for your use case, please let us know by emailing Support@Verified.inc, and we'll prioritize accordingly.
:::
