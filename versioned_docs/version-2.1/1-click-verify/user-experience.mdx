It's in the name: _verify is what we do._ We are experts in how to optimize onboarding flows to maximize conversion and give users the best possible experience. We've helped our customers onboard — and delight! — millions of new users. The user experience described in this guide is the result of countless hours of research, design, testing, and iteration by our team. 

:::tip Use Our SDK to Get This UX
**Our SDK implements this user experience, and we strongly recommend you use it.** If you instead choose to use our **API**, we recommend you implement a user experience similar to this one.
:::

Full details are below, but the basic user experience is:
1. (if autofill available) **Trigger autofill** (e.g. click a button)
2. (if necessary) **Enter phone number**
3. (if necessary) **Enter SMS verification code**
