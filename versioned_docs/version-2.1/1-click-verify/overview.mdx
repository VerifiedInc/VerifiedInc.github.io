## About 1-Click Verify

**1-Click Verify is our dedicated solution for autofilling and verifying user phone numbers** (and soon emails). Collecting a user's verified phone number is necessary to use our flagship [1-Click Signup](/1-click-signup) product, so 1-Click Verify provides a powerful and easy to integrate solution.


## Phone Carrier Compliance

Autofilling verified phone numbers and sending SMS in the US requires onerous compliance reviews by phone carriers. We handle all of this complexity, so you don't have to.


## Benefits

- **Magically autofill verified user phone numbers:** After years of effort, we've implemented a way to autofill verified phone numbers _with no user input!_ This is the best user experience on the planet. Painless, frictionless phone verification.
- **Easily verify user phone numbers:** 1-Click Verify makes it extremely easy to verify user phone numbers, with minimal integration work.
- **Seamlessly integrate with 1-Click Signup and 1-Click Health:** Phone verification is the prerequisite for using our flagship [1-Click Signup](/1-click-signup) product, so 1-Click Verify can smoothly flow into 1-Click Signup (which can then flow into 1-Click Health).


## Coverage

1-Click Verify works for 99% of US adults (264M people): anyone with a cell phone!

That said, coverage is different for the two 1-Click Verify channels:
- **Autofill:** The autofill channel is currently only available a minority of the time. The exact coverage will depend on your use case — the main factors being whether a user is on WiFi vs. cell data and whether their phone carrier supports autofill — but we typically see it work for about 1 in 6 sessions. 
- **SMS:** The SMS channel works for 99% of users — anyone with a cell phone. It uses industry standard SMS verification codes.

:::tip
Even if the autofill channel only works for a small percentage of your users, it will substantially boost overall conversion (and user delight!) in your onboarding flow, so we highly recommend it!
:::

:::important
**We currently only cover people in the United States,** but we're soon adding new geographies. If there are particular geographies that would be most helpful for your use case, please let us know by emailing Support@Verified.inc, and we'll prioritize accordingly.
:::
