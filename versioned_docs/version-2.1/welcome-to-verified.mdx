Welcome! At Verified, we help companies like yours onboard real people faster. 

**[1-Click Signup](/1-click-signup) is the _fastest_ way to sign up new users.** From just a phone number, Verified can autofill a user's data so they can share it in less than 10 seconds. _Try it yourself [here](https://1-click.demo.verified.inc/register?product=1ClickSignup)!_

**[Text to Signup](/text-to-signup) is a marketing _superpower_.** It lets you create magical ads that convert verified users instantly, both online and offline. _Try it yourself [here](https://1-click.demo.verified.inc/register?product=TextToSignup)!_

**When combined, these provide a full stack growth solution.** 1-Click Signup is for _onboarding_: it helps you convert users who already want to sign up. Text to Signup is for _marketing_: it helps you reach users and make them want to sign up.
