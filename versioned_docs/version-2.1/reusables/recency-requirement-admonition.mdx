<Admonition type="info" title="Recency Requirement">
    **You must make the `PATCH /1-click/{uuid}` call within 30 minutes of having made the `POST /1-click` that returned `uuid`.** Otherwise, you'll get an error that the 1-Click request expired ([`OCE014`](/api-reference/errors#oce014) error code).
</Admonition>
